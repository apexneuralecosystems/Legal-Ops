SUPPLIER CONTRACT DISPUTE - BRIEF SUMMARY

Case Type: Breach of Supply Agreement
Court: High Court of Malaya
Jurisdiction: Kuala Lumpur

PLAINTIFF: Premium Foods Distribution Sdn Bhd
DEFENDANT: Fresh Farm Supplies Sdn Bhd

FACTS:

1. Supply Agreement dated 1 January 2024 for delivery of fresh vegetables
2. Contract value: RM 500,000 over 6 months
3. Weekly deliveries every Monday by 8:00 AM
4. Quality standards: Grade A fresh produce only

BREACH:

1. Defendant failed to deliver on 15 occasions (March-August 2024)
2. Delivered substandard Grade C produce on 8 occasions
3. Late deliveries on 12 occasions (arrived after 2:00 PM)
4. Caused plaintiff to lose major hotel contracts due to unreliable supply

DAMAGES:

1. Cost of emergency alternative suppliers (premium rates): RM 85,000
2. Loss of hotel contracts (3 major clients): RM 450,000
3. Storage costs for rejected goods: RM 8,000
4. Reputational damage and customer compensation: RM 50,000

Total Claim: RM 593,000 plus legal costs and interest

RELIEF SOUGHT:
- Judgment for RM 593,000
- Termination of supply agreement
- Costs and interest

Prepared: 1 September 2024
