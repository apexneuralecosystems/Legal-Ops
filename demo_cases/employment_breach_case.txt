EMPLOYMENT CONTRACT DISPUTE - CASE SUMMARY

PARTIES:
Plaintiff: Tech Solutions Sdn Bhd (Company Registration No. 201234567-X)
Address: Level 12, Menara KLCC, Kuala Lumpur

Defendant: Ahmad bin Abdullah
Address: No. 45, Jalan Ampang, Kuala Lumpur

CASE BACKGROUND:

1. On 1 January 2023, the Plaintiff entered into an Employment Agreement with the Defendant for the position of Senior Software Engineer.

2. The employment contract stipulated:
   - Monthly salary: RM 8,000
   - Contract duration: 2 years (until 31 December 2024)
   - Notice period: 3 months
   - Non-compete clause: 1 year in similar industry

3. On 15 August 2024, the Defendant verbally informed the Plaintiff that he would be resigning effective immediately to join a competitor company, Innovative Tech Sdn Bhd.

4. The Defendant did not serve the required 3-month notice period as stipulated in the employment contract.

5. The Defendant left the company on 20 August 2024, taking with him confidential client information and proprietary source code.

6. The Defendant began working for Innovative Tech Sdn Bhd on 25 August 2024, in direct violation of the non-compete clause.

BREACH OF CONTRACT:

The Defendant has breached the Employment Agreement in the following ways:
a) Failed to provide the required 3-month notice period
b) Violated the non-compete clause by joining a competitor
c) Misappropriated confidential information and trade secrets

DAMAGES CLAIMED:

1. Salary for 3 months notice period not served: RM 24,000
2. Recruitment and training costs for replacement: RM 15,000
3. Loss of business due to breach of non-compete: RM 50,000
4. Damages for misappropriation of confidential information: RM 100,000
5. Legal costs and interest

TOTAL CLAIM: RM 189,000 plus costs and interest

PRAYERS:

1. Judgment against the Defendant for the sum of RM 189,000
2. Injunction restraining the Defendant from working for competitors
3. Order for return of all confidential documents and data
4. Costs of this action
5. Interest at 8% per annum from date of breach
6. Such further or other relief as the Court deems just

EVIDENCE AVAILABLE:
- Original Employment Agreement dated 1 January 2023
- Email correspondence regarding resignation
- Company records showing early termination
- Evidence of employment with competitor
- Client complaint letters
- Financial statements showing loss of business

Date: 1 September 2024
Case prepared for filing in the High Court of Malaya at Kuala Lumpur
